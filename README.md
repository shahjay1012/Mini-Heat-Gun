## Overview
The **Chandler Tools HG603D Mini Heat Gun** is a compact, dual-temperature **350W hot air tool** designed for precision crafting, DIY projects, small repairs, and controlled heating applications. Manufactured in Taiwan, this corded electric heat gun operates at **120V / 60Hz** and provides reliable, focused airflow for detail-oriented work.

---

## Purpose and Application
This mini heat gun is widely used across **crafting, electronics, DIY, and small-scale repair tasks**. It is ideal for:

- Shrink-wrapping and heat-shrink tubing  
- Resin art, embossing, and vinyl crafting  
- Softening adhesives and removing stickers  
- Drying paint, clay, or small surface coatings  
- Precision heating in electronics and hobby projects  

With **dual temperature settings**, the HG603D supports both delicate materials and higher-temperature applications, ensuring controlled and efficient heating. Its compact form makes it perfect for situations where full-size heat guns are impractical.
